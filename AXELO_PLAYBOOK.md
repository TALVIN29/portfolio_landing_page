## 1. BRAND

**Name:** AXELO
**AX** = action, cutting edge. **ELO** = velocity, elevation, momentum.

**One-line:** "We find broken business operations and turn them into automated systems that run without you."

**Tagline options:** "Broken process? We fix that." | "Operations that run without you." | "Designed to outlive its creator."

**Story:** Our first production system was a transaction monitoring engine for a fintech company — but the principle behind it drives everything we build: zero hardcoded logic, business-team ownership, minimum 10-page technical + operations documentation, graceful degradation. That principle applies whether we're automating compliance, e-commerce operations, or HR onboarding. Every growing company hits a wall — spreadsheets multiply, copy-paste jobs pile up, the team spends hours on work that doesn't matter. AXELO exists to tear down that wall and build systems YOU own, understand, and can run without us.

**Values:** Transparency. Ownership. Documentation. Simplicity. Evolution.

**WHY:** "I come alive when I take something broken and manual and make it work automatically. The transformation is what drives me."

**Voice:** Confident but not arrogant. Technical but accessible. Show, don't tell. Numbers, not adjectives.

## 2. BUSINESS MODEL

### The Active Automation Architect

We actively find broken processes in public, diagnose them for free in content, and offer personalized fixes through a diagnostic + implementation package.

Not a passive consultancy waiting for clients. Not a template shop selling generic files. AXELO shows up where the crowd is, cooks in front of them, makes it personal.

**Revenue = 4 Layers (all USD):**

| Layer | What | Price | Type |
|-------|------|-------|------|
| 1. FREE | Public diagnosis (LinkedIn, Dev.to, Reddit) | $0 | Marketing engine |
| 2. ENTRY | Premium templates on Gumroad | $29–$99 | Passive |
| 3. CORE | Diagnostic + adapted implementation | $750–$8,000 | Active |
| 4. RECURRING | Managed operations (host + maintain) | $500–$2,000/mo | Cash cow |

### The Flywheel

Public content → attracts people with same problem → some buy templates → some want custom help → completed project = next case study → case study = next content post → cycle repeats. Managed clients fund more content. Each cycle strengthens proof, expands audience, stabilizes revenue.

### Pricing Rules
- **ALWAYS price at 5–10% of the annual value the automation creates for the client.**
- Calculate value in the diagnostic. Show the calculation in the proposal.
- Templates: charm pricing ($29, $49, $79). Services: round anchors ($750, $2,500, $5,000). Always 3 options (middle = target). Monthly pricing for recurring ($500/mo < $6,000/yr psychologically).

### Why People Pay When Free Exists
Free template = raw JSON, figure it out. Paid = full docs + video walkthrough + test cases + AXELO quality stamp + 30-minute to production. The difference isn't the code. It's the confidence and speed.

### Managed Operations Clarification
Managed Operations is an OPTIONAL SERVICE LAYER — not a dependency. The client's system works without it. "Zero-maintenance" applies to what we build; Managed Ops is convenience and insurance.

When a managed client wants to leave: AXELO provides a migration guide, exports all configs, and hands over all documentation within 5 business days. Client owns everything. Exit is clean.

Sales channels: Gumroad (templates), direct invoice (services), Lemon Squeezy (alternative to Gumroad).

## 6. MARKETING — "COOK IN PUBLIC"

### Principle
Every content piece = public diagnosis of a broken process. Not "5 automation tips." Instead: "Here's how [role] at [company type] wastes 10hrs/week and the exact workflow that fixes it."

### Pipeline
```
Content (broken process diagnosis)
  → Reader: "that's MY problem"
    → Clicks AXELO landing page
      → Path A: buys template (self-serve)
      → Path B: fills diagnostic form (wants help — 48hr response SLA)
      → Path C: follows for more (future buyer)
        → Completed project → case study → next content → cycle
```

### Content Targets (measurable)
- 2 LinkedIn posts/week
- 3 Reddit/forum answers/week (r/n8n, r/automation, n8n community)
- 1 long-form article/month (Dev.to or Hashnode)
- Diagnostic form responses: within 48 hours

### Calendar
**Launch (weeks 1–4):** 1 LinkedIn post/week, Case Study #1 on Dev.to, 2 free templates, 1 Loom walkthrough.
**Sustain:** 2 LinkedIn posts/week (1 broad + 1 fintech), 1 template/month, 1 article/month.

### Channels (introvert-ranked)
1. LinkedIn (write, don't network) — 2 hrs/week
2. n8n Community (help, share) — 1 hr/week
3. Dev.to/Hashnode (long-form) — 2 hrs/month
4. Reddit (answer in r/n8n, r/automation) — 30 min/week
5. YouTube (screen recording, no face) — DEFERRED to Month 6+
6. Gumroad (product listing) — setup once
